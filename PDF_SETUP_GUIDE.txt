QUICK GUIDE: Setting Up PDF Download Links
===========================================

After uploading your website to GitHub, follow these simple steps:

STEP 1: Upload PDF Files to Repository Root
--------------------------------------------
Upload these 4 PDF files to the SAME directory as index.html:
  ✓ AI_Hazards_Report.pdf
  ✓ Social_Media_Impacts.pdf  
  ✓ VR_Report.pdf
  ✓ Video_Game_Impacts.pdf

STEP 2: That's it!
------------------
The PDF links are already configured as relative paths.
Once the PDFs are in the root directory, all buttons will work automatically.

No need to edit any files or update any links!

File Structure Should Look Like This:
--------------------------------------
your-repository/
├── index.html
├── category1.html
├── category2.html
├── ...
├── AI_Hazards_Report.pdf          ← Upload here
├── Social_Media_Impacts.pdf        ← Upload here
├── VR_Report.pdf                   ← Upload here
├── Video_Game_Impacts.pdf          ← Upload here
└── css/
    └── style.css

Testing:
--------
After uploading, visit your live site and click the PDF buttons
to ensure they open/download correctly.
